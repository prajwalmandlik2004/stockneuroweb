Himachal Trip

Day 1 - Mumbai - Chandigarh Train

  • Assemble at Bandra Terminus by 10.30 HRS to Board Train for Chandigarh by 12.00HRS
  • Full Day in Train
  • Overnight Enroute in Train

Day 2 - Chandigarh - Kasol

  • Arrive Chandigarh by 16.00HRS and Depart to Kasol by Bus
  • Overnight Journey in Bus
  • Dinner would be served Enroute

Day 3 - Kasol Sightseeing

  • Reach Kasol, Breakfast and Check in to Camps
  • After Lunch Proceed to Visit Manikaran Temple and Hot Water Spring
  • Free Time for Shopping at Local Market
  • Return back to Camp, followed by Bonfire, Dinner and Overnight Stay at the Camp

Day 4 - Kasol - Manali

  • Early Morning Checkout and Proceed to Manali, Breakfast will be served Enroute then Check in at Hotel
  • On the way Enjoy White Water Rafting
  • Leisure time to Explore Old Manali or You can do Cafe Hopping at own Back to Hotel
  • Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 5 - Manali Sightseeing

  • After Breakfast Proceed to Visit Hidimba Devi Temple and Solang Valley
  • Snow Point (Adventure Activities at own cost)
  • Evening you can Visit Key Monastery and Manali Mall road then back to Hotel
  • Dinner and Overnight Stay at the Hotel

Day 6 - Manali to Amritsar

  • Breakfast, Check out and proceed for Amritsar
  • On the way visit Pharma Industry at Mandi
  • Lunch and Dinner would be served enroute
  • Overnight Journey in Bus

Day 7 - Amritsar Sightseeing

  • Arrive Amritsar and Check-in at the Hotel
  • After Breakfast, Proceed to visit Golden Temple and Jallianwala Baug
  • After Lunch Proceed for Wagha border
  • Evening free time for shopping at Local Market
  • DJ Night Followed by Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 8 - Train Back to Mumbai

  • Early Morning Checkout and Depart to Amritsar Railway station to board Train back to Mumbai
  • Full day in Train
  • Overnight Enroute in Train
