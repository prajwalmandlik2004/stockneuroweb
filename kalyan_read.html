Kalyan Jewellers

Kalyan Jewellers' Q3 FY 2024 Highlights:

Impressive Revenue Growth: Kalyan Jewellers experienced a remarkable 40% overall revenue growth, signaling strong market demand. Operating Momentum: Robust on-ground operations led to a 6% revenue increase in the Middle East and positive same-store-sales growth in key Indian markets. Strategic Expansion: 22 new showrooms added, with 16 operating under the Franchisee Owned Franchisee Operated model. Middle East Success: The Middle East segment contributed 13% to consolidated revenue, highlighting business success in the region
