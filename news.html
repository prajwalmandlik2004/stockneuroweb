Sanghi

Sanghi Industries shares plunged 10% on the NSE as it entered a master supply agreement with Ambuja Cements, its parent company, selling all output at a fixed 10% markup over production cost. Analysts anticipate this move will significantly limit Sanghi's profitability, capping it at the modest markup. Ambuja Cements, holding a 54.51% stake, acquired Sanghi Industries in August.

ADANI GROUP

The project, powered by renewable energy, aims to boost green infrastructure, create 20,000 jobs, and aligns with Mumbai's goal to achieve 60% renewable energy supply by 2027.

Vodafone Idea

Vodafone Idea faces a Rs 13.16 crore penalty imposed by the GST additional commissioner's office in Chandigarh for alleged wrongful transition of CENVAT credit to the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime. The telecom firm, grappling with financial challenges, disclosed the order on January 10 in a regulatory filing.

In June 2023, tax authorities surveyed Shree Cement, revealing wrongful claims totaling Rs 8,500 crore from Apr '14 to Mar '23.
