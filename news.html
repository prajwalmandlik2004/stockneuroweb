What is stock market in simple words?

The stock market is where investors but and sell shares of companies. It's a set of exchanges where companies issue shares and other securities for trading.



Shree Cement

The tax department alleged wrongful claims and deductions based on the June 2023 survey.

Vodafone Idea

Vodafone Idea faces a Rs 13.16 crore penalty imposed by the GST additional commissioner's office in Chandigarh for alleged wrongful transition of CENVAT credit to the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime. The telecom firm, grappling with financial challenges, disclosed the order on January 10 in a regulatory filing.

Coal India

Union Minister Pralhad Joshi asserted in an exclusive interview with Moneycontrol that the government plans to retain ownership of all public sector undertakings (PSUs) under the Ministry of Coal. His statement comes amid speculation that the Ministry of Finance might sell up to a 10 percent stake in certain PSUs, including NLC India.
