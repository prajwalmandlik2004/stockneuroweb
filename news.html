Shree Cement

In June 2023, tax authorities surveyed Shree Cement, revealing wrongful claims totaling Rs 8,500 crore from Apr '14 to Mar '23. On January 5, the company's shares plummeted due to reports of a Rs 4,000-crore tax order by the income tax department. The tax department alleged wrongful claims and deductions based on the June 2023 survey. Shree Cement refuted the claims, calling them speculative. Despite the denial, the stock dropped 4.4% to Rs 27,088 on the BSE. Earlier, the company announced a brand revamp, aiming to enhance customer connection and offer unique experiences with 'Bangur' as the master brand. Neeraj Akhoury, Managing Director, highlighted the strategic alignment with evolving customer sentiments
