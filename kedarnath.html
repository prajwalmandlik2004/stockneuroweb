Kedarnath Yatra

2024 Batch Details:

  • may - 12th, 18th, 24th
  • june - 5th, 11th, 17th
  • july - 3rd, 10th, 17th
  • aug - 11th, 18th, 25th
  • sept - 4th, 12th, 15th, 20th , 28th
  • oct - 2nd, 5th, 10th, 18th

Day 01 mumbai - new delhi

  • assemble at bandra terminus at 10.am to board train 12925 paschim express departing to new delhi 11.30 am.
  • overnight enroute in train

Day 02 new delhi – haridwar

  • arrive new delhi by 10.40 am.
  • depart to haridwar by bus.
  • evening arrive haridwar & visit ganga aarti at har ki pauri.
  • transfer to hotel, dinner & overnight stay at hotel.

Day 03 haridwar – guptkashi

  • after breakfast check-out for guptkashi by bus.
  • enroute enjoy devprayag sangam.
  • evening arrive guptkashi, dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 04 guptkashi – kedarnath

  • after breakfast proceed to kedarnath (18kms trek from sonprayag) reach temple & check-in hotel.
  • maha aarti, dinner & overnight stay at hotel.

Day 05 kedarnath - guptkashi

  • after breakfast take lord shiva blessings & start trekking down towards sonprayag.
  • reach guptkashi, dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 06 haridwar - rishikesh

  • after breakfast check out & depart to rishikesh.
  • evening arrive rishikesh & check in at the hotel/camp stay.
  • dinner & overnight stay at the hotel/camp stay

Day 07 rishikesh - new delhi

  • after breakfast check-out & proceed for river rafting (own cost) & lakshmanjhula.
  • evening free time for shopping at local market.
  • depart to new delhi railway station.
  • overnight enroute in bus.

Day 08 new delhi to mumbai

  • arrive new delhi station to board 12904 golden temple mail depart to mumbai by 04.00 am.
  • full day in train.
  • arrive mumbai by 23.30hrs with happy memories

Highlight of the tour

  • 8 fun days, 3 cities and 1 wonderful snowy state.
  • visit hari ki pauri and witness the mesmerizing ganga aarti at ganga ghat.
  • witness the sangam of two heavenly rivers, alakananda and bhagirathi at devprayag.
  • explore & witness the mesmerizing view of kedarnath temple.
  • fun & adventure trek of 22 km in the snow to reach kedarnath temple.
  • visit ram jhula and enjoy white water river rafting at rishikesh (at your own cost
